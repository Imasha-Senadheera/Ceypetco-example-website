Fuel pass

Follow these simple steps to register for the service and to receive yoyr allocated fuel quota

Step-1     Visit the portal via ceypetco.gov.lk and click "Registration"

Step-2     Add your NIC number and mobile number & other details

Step-3     Fill out the required vehicle details

Step-4     After filling all the details click the "Submit" button

Step-5     The QR code will be displayed the screen (Ps: As display below)

Step-6     Take a screenshot or get a printout of the original QR code

Step-7     Present the QR code to the fuel station to obtain the Service
